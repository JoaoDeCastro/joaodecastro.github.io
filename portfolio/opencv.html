Mount your music

Reality-based-interaction project to help kids understand some principle concepts of music notation

The idea was to make a 3D real-life version of music notation, where the user would be able to point it to the camera and the software will recognize the music notes
This image shows the assembly plan for the project

This is what I called the "half sphere" which represents the notes in the music notation

This is the product after 3D printing. I was able to mount it with some things to improve. Which is being implemented at this moment

This is the final concept piece. I am working on it to print more models for testing, also working on the software

Using convolution neural network algorithm the model is already recognizing the pieces. Further training is being done
